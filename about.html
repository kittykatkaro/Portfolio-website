About me

My Name is Karo Ogorek. I am currently a full-stack web developer in training at Career Foundry.
Originally from Poland, I am now based in Germany. I speak Polish, German and English fluently. Additionally, I speak Japanese at a beginner level (JLPT N4).

Download my CV

Before starting my training as a web developer, I worked for four years in a German bank. This includes the 2 1/2 year training period. I learned the basics of the banking industry, financial accounting, and business administration.
Deciding that this was not my passion, I enrolled at the Free University in Berlin, studying East-Asian Art History with a focus on Japan. Mandatory for the degree was an internship in a company related to the field of study. I completed my internship at the Samurai Museum Berlin with a job offer at the end. I worked for three years in the museum where I was responsible for writing descriptions for the objects and exhibition, database management, and digital exhibition maintenance. Additionally, I was responsible for the object pictures (cataloging, editing, and uploading to the database).
